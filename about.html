About us

Zionwol Solutions LTD. (RC 1650253) is a technological company established to provide world class engineering services for Nigeria populace. We offer a range of services covering power and energy, information technology, security and communication. We are one of the leading renewable energy companies in Nigeria, providing a steady power supply to homes and businesses using clean energy especially from the sun. We do installation, repair and maintenance of solar and inverter, CCTV, INTERCOM, Satellite TV and electric fence. We also offer services in IT Solutions, Web designing and computer networking.

At Zionwol Solutions LTD, we provide turnkey Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) and Operational & Maintenance (O&M) services for call our clients anywhere in Nigeria and sub-Saharan Africa. We do maintain a clear ground on quality service delivery that will satisfy our customers.

In our quest to advocate for the nation’s go green, skill acquisition and youth empowerment, our company provide solar power solutions and training programs on Solar & Inverter, CCTV, INTERCOM, Satellite TV, Electric Fencing, Web Designing, and many more.
